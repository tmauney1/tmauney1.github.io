1. The project manager is responsible for making sure the project is completed on time, within budget, and has all the correct features and parts. They are in charge of the business aspect of the multimedia project. The project designer is in charge of the creation of the actually project. They are responsible for the construction, design, look, feel, and functionality of the project.


2. A storyboard is a series of sketches of the major screens of the multimedia project. A storyboard can help easily show the projects design and functions to the client. It serves as a rough draft to allow all parties involved to have a general idea of what the finished product will look like and be able to do. Therefore any changes or questions about the project can be clarified before investing too much time and money into developing the project.
